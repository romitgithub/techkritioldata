There is a competition associated with the workshop sessions of Webpreneurship in which students generate a business plan and then they implement the plan to design a working website of their own. Structure of competition is as follows :-


Round 1


Students will be divided in a team of four to make a business plan. They need to submit their B-Plan online as per the format provided by RoboIndia Technologies.


Round 2


Design the implementation of the b-plan on the subdomain provided by RoboIndia technologies or on any other domain/sub-domain.
Submit your live website link along with a presentation on your B-plan online.


Round 3


Get mentored by Venture Capitalists. (Supported by E Cell, IIT Kanpur)
Participate directly into the Finals of IDEAS during Techkriti'15, IIT Kanpur.

In its efforts to fulfill the social obligation of serving the society along with technical advancement, IIT Kanpur, one of the premier technical institute of India, Techkriti, the annual technical and entrepreneurial festival of IIT Kanpur and SparkLab Engineering System Pvt. Ltd. presents the Robotics Challenge, with a vision to motivate people all over the world to indulge in their passion for robotics.

The workshops will also open an opportunity for the students to be a part of the "IARC" during Techkriti 2015 at IIT Kanpur.

This total Event is Divided into 4 Rounds:


Stage 1 (Training Round to be held at College):

  • Training/Workshops will be held at different centers all across India.
  • The Duration of the Workshop will be of 2 Days(6-7 hours each day)
  • The Workshop will be delivered by SparkLab Engineering System Pvt. Ltd.

Stage 2 (Competition Round to be held at College on the second day of workshop):

  • Just after the workshop, a mega competition will be held.
  • Winners will be awarded with "Certificate of Merit" & will be invited to participate in Final Round

Stage 3 (Final Round of TECHNSOIA to be held at IIT-Kanpur)

  • Winners of Innovative Centers are required to participate in the Final Round of "TECHNOSIA-2015" in the month of March-2015 at IIT-Kanpur during Techkriti'15.
  • Winner of this Final Round will win "TECHNOSIA-2015" championship and will be awarded and Excellence by TECHNOSIA-2015.
  • A Certificate of Excellence will also be awarded to the Winning Team.

Stage 4 (Final Round of iARC to be held at IIT-Kanpur)

  • Winners of TECHNOSIA are promoted to participate in a final round of iARC.
  • Winners of iARC will get the prize money of $3200 (USD).
  • A certificate of Honor will be awarded to winning team by Techkriti-2015
  • The fee per participant is 1200 INR (non-refundable).
  • Free kit will be given to each group of 4- 5 members.

Spark Lab Engineering System Pvt. Ltd is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified organization established under the company act 1956. It has been setup by an experienced team of Engineers to carry out R&D and design work in the field of electronics, electrical engineering design, Computer Science and associated areas, many of which have come out as an identification of opportunities.
